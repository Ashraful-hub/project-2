BMW Series 1

Category: Subcompact Luxury Hatchback Overview: The 1 Series is the smallest and most affordable vehicle in BMW’s lineup. It's known for its sporty handling and premium features, making it a popular choice for those looking for a compact, luxurious car. Notable Features: Front-wheel drive (FWD) or all-wheel drive (AWD), compact size, dynamic driving experience. Engine Options: Typically comes with 3-cylinder and 4-cylinder engines, with mild-hybrid options in some variants.

BMW 3 Series

Category: Compact Luxury Sedan Overview: One of BMW’s most iconic models, the 3 Series is known for its balanced performance, precision handling, and luxurious interior. It has been a benchmark for sports sedans for decades. Notable Features: Rear-wheel drive (RWD) or AWD, available in sedan, wagon (Touring), and hybrid configurations. Engine Options: Inline-4, inline-6, and hybrid variants (330e, etc.). High-performance M models (M3) also available. Key Highlights: Standard tech features include iDrive, Apple CarPlay, and advanced safety systems.

BMW 7 Series

Category: Mid-Size Luxury Sedan Overview: The 5 Series is a more refined version of the 3 Series, offering more space, luxury, and advanced technology. It's a favorite among executives looking for a blend of comfort and performance. Notable Features: Available in sedan and wagon (Touring) variants, both RWD and AWD. Engine Options: Inline-4, inline-6, and V8 engines, including mild-hybrid options. M variants (M5) offer high-performance engines. Key Highlights: Luxurious interior, intuitive tech, impressive driving performance, adaptive suspension.

BMW 4 Series

Category: Compact Luxury Coupe/Cabriolet Overview: A sportier, more stylish variant of the 3 Series, the 4 Series comes in coupe, convertible, and gran coupe body styles. It offers a more focused driving experience. Notable Features: Performance-oriented handling, aggressive styling, rear-wheel drive or AWD, sportier suspension. Engine Options: Inline-4 and inline-6 engines, with performance M variants (M4). Key Highlights: Excellent driving dynamics, high-quality interior, strong emphasis on sportiness.

BMW 5 Series

Category: Mid-Size Luxury Sedan Overview: The 5 Series is a more refined version of the 3 Series, offering more space, luxury, and advanced technology. It's a favorite among executives looking for a blend of comfort and performance. Notable Features: Available in sedan and wagon (Touring) variants, both RWD and AWD. Engine Options: Inline-4, inline-6, and V8 engines, including mild-hybrid options. M variants (M5) offer high-performance engines. Key Highlights: Luxurious interior, intuitive tech, impressive driving performance, adaptive suspension.
